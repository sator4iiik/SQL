are printed -- 3 originals for the bank, and 3 
copies for the customer. Here is the list of all 
the checks named 'Operation'

------------------------+------------------------
            3           |       Check3
------------------------+------------------------
            4           |       Check4
------------------------+------------------------
            5           |       Check5
------------------------+------------------------
            6           |       Check6
------------------------+------------------------

are given to the customer.

=================================================
